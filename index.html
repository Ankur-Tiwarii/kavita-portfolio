About Me

Designing Textiles with Passion & Precision

I am a textile and print designer specializing in creating intricate digital textile panels, surface patterns, and garment-ready prints that tell stories through color and form.

My work spans across multiple domains of textile design — from ornate heritage-inspired panels with rich jewelry motifs to contemporary surface patterns and print-on-product applications for fashion. Each design is crafted with meticulous attention to detail, color harmony, and production feasibility.

I draw inspiration from diverse sources: traditional Indian artistry, botanical elements, Art Deco geometry, global textile traditions like Ikat, and the natural world. My goal is to create designs that are not only visually stunning but also production-ready for the textile and fashion industry.
