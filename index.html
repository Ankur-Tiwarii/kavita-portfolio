About

Designing textiles with
passion & precision

I specialize in creating intricate digital textile panels, surface patterns, and garment-ready prints that tell stories through color and form.

My inspiration flows from traditional Indian artistry, botanical elements, Art Deco geometry, and global textile traditions like Ikat. Every design balances visual beauty with production feasibility.
